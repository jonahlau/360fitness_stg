PHYSIOTHERAPY


Prehabilitation - Prevention is better than Cure

We can simply define it as a personalised training regime with sport specific exercise to prevent or minimise the risk of injury. Our physiotherapist and coach first analyse ones posture, joint alignment, flexibility, muscle control, biomechanics, core stability and movement patterns, then evaluate the risks of the sport itself. It in turn comes up with Program

Physiotherapy - Restoration of your bodily function

We offer one-on-one physiotherapy service for musculoskeletal disorders from spine to all peripheral joint, sport and post surgical rehabilitation as well as chest physiotherapy. Our therapist does not reply on electrotherapy modalities, instead endorses a hands-on approach to treatment like Gausha, cupping and other myofascial release methods. We focus on symptomatic relief with restoration of the proper biomechanics of your body. Your physiotherapist will prescribe corrective exercises on postural reeducation, joint mobility and self help soft tissue release as your home exercise. All these prepare you for the next phase of rehabilitation.

Active Rehabilitation- Back to the Field with Better Performance

This is gym-based program tailored for those clients no longer requiring acute care treatment but have ongoing physical complaints with respect to their original injuries. Exercise, education and evaluation are the foundation of this program. Our physiotherapist will come alongside with you and work closely with your or our trainer to design, evaluate and revise your training protocol. It aims to optimize your body's performance in the specific activities you participate in and to achieve more than you thought you were capable of.


RECOVERY

If you are challenging yourself hard in your workout, you are likely experiencing muscle soreness and tightness after the training. Massage and other soft tissue release method effectively enhance circulation to muscles, which is ideal for post workout recovery
